about

Hey! I'm Tanya - a full stack developer from Atlanta, GA. I'm high-spirited, insatiably curious, and always good for a laugh. I love coding, my three dogs (Walter, Doug, and Tesla), and learning anything new - just for the fun of it (most recently wire-wrapping jewelry and basic Japanese!).

I got my start in web development when I was 14. I was a huge fan of Neopets, a website where you have a virtual pets, play mini games, build its skills, and earn accessories. You could have your own 'guild' and have people join. I wanted to customize my guild page, so I taught myself basic HTML, CSS, and a little Photoshop to make it happen. I've since (thankfully) expanded my knowledge in development technologies, and most recently went though Georgia Tech's Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp.
