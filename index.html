full stack developer

Hey, I'm Tanya - a full stack developer from Atlanta. I'm high-spirited, decisive, and unabashedly myself. A serial hobbyist, I'm obsessed with learning and enthusiastic about trying new things. Being someone that loves to be creative, take things apart and put them back together just to see how they work, and always reading about new tech and science innovations, it's like programming just gets me, you know?

When I was 13, I wanted to style my Neopets guild page, which led me to the first time I ever coded and general web design, tinkering with CSS, HTML, and Photoshop to design my own background image. I've since (obviously) expanded my knowledge in development technologies and use them professionally.
